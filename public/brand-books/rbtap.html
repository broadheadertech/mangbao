/ 01 — The Brand

Bawat tap,
may digital access.

RBTap is the everyday digital payment hub for provincial Philippines — built on the trust of community banking, delivered with the speed of modern fintech. From cash-in to QR pay, bills to remittance, RBTap is where the bayan goes to move money simply, safely, and without barriers.

/ 02 — Logo System

A mark built for
kiosks, screens & signage.

/ 05 — Typography

Geometric. Readable.
Mass-market ready.

Archivo carries the brand voice — bold, geometric, unmistakably modern, designed to read from across a barangay plaza. Hanken Grotesk handles the everyday text. JetBrains Mono adds technical precision to receipts, data, and labels.
